Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Denterview™ collects, uses, and protects your information when you use denterview.com and related services including denterviewai.com. By accessing our site or purchasing our products, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information — We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including your name, email address, payment information (processed by third parties), personal statement content submitted for review, and any messages you send us.

Non-Personal Information — We may collect IP address, browser type, pages visited, time on site, and device information to improve our services.

AI Interview Data — When using Denterview AI, video and audio recordings are processed to generate feedback. This data is used solely to provide the service and generate your performance report.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process transactions and deliver purchased products and services
  • To deliver personal statement feedback to reviewers and back to you
  • To provide AI mock interview analysis and performance reports
  • To respond to customer inquiries and support requests
  • To send occasional updates or promotional emails (you may opt out at any time)
  • To improve site functionality and user experience

3. Personal Statement Submissions

When you submit a personal statement for review, your document is shared with an independent dental student reviewer for the sole purpose of providing feedback. Reviewers are bound by confidentiality expectations. Your personal statement will not be shared, published, or used for any other purpose.

4. Payment Processing

We do not store credit card or payment information. All payments are securely processed by Payhip in compliance with their privacy and security policies. Please review Payhip's privacy policy at payhip.com for details on how payment data is handled.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only with trusted service providers (payment processors, email platforms) who help us operate our business — only to the extent necessary to provide our services.

6. Data Retention

We retain customer information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Personal statements submitted for review are not retained beyond the service period unless required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Cookies

We use cookies and analytics tools (including Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Opting out of Google Analytics tracking is available at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

8. Children's Privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of such information, it will be deleted promptly.

9. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To opt out of email communications, click unsubscribe in any email we send. For any privacy requests, contact us at denterviews@gmail.com.

10. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The effective date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of any updates.

11. Contact

Questions? Contact us at denterviews@gmail.com or visit denterview.com
