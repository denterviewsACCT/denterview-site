Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Denterview™ collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website, denterview.com, and related services. By accessing our site or purchasing our products, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information
We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Name

  • Payment information (via third-party processors)
  • Any messages or inquiries you send us

Non-Personal Information
We may collect non-personal data such as:

  • IP address

This helps us improve the site experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

We do not store any credit card information. All payments are securely processed by third-party platforms (Payhip) in compliance with their privacy and security policies.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We may share data with trusted service providers (like email platforms or payment processors) who help us operate our business — but only to the extent necessary.

We may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and collect analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Email Communication

By purchasing or signing up, you may receive transactional or promotional emails. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the link in our emails.

7. Data Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure. You use the Site and submit information at your own risk.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of such information, it will be deleted.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access or request deletion of your personal data
