🦷 About Us

Built by students
who actually got in.

Denterview was created by current dental school students who went through the same stressful, confusing, and competitive application process you're facing now.

Why we built this

Like many applicants, we struggled to find affordable, high-quality prep materials for dental school interviews — especially content that didn't feel generic, robotic, or out of touch. Most guides were either outdated, AI-generated fluff, or written by people who hadn't been through the modern AADSAS cycle themselves.

So we started compiling the most authentic, effective strategies we used to succeed — along with dozens of real interview questions, polished answer frameworks, and tips that actually work in today's admissions landscape.

Everything on Denterview is original, hand-crafted, and based on what helped the creators earn multiple interviews and acceptances at competitive U.S. dental schools.

What makes us different

No filler. No fake testimonials. No unrealistic promises. Just the tools we wish we had when we were applying.

Whether you're a first-gen applicant, reapplying after a tough cycle, or aiming for your top-choice school — Denterview is here to help you show up confident, prepared, and real.

Our products
