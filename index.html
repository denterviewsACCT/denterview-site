Kira Talent Prep

Getting a Kira interview?
We've got you covered.

More dental schools are using Kira Talent for asynchronous video interviews — and most students have no idea what to expect. Kira is entirely behavioral: you record yourself answering questions on a timer with no second chances. The good news is that behavioral answers are exactly what we train.

  • Kira questions are almost entirely behavioral — our 73-question guide covers them directly
  • Our mock interview platform simulates timed, on-camera recording — identical to the Kira format
  • Get instant feedback on eye contact and pacing — both critical for async video interviews
  • Learn STAR-format storytelling to nail any behavioral prompt under time pressure
Prep for Kira →

What to expect from a Kira interview

  • Timed responsesTypically 1–3 minutes per question. You don't get to re-record, so preparation is everything.
  • Behavioral questions"Tell me about a time you..." — every question. Structure and stories are your preparation strategy.
  • Asynchronous — no interviewerYou're recording solo to a camera. Presence, eye contact, and calm delivery matter more than ever.
  • Reviewed by admissions committeesSchools watch your recording when evaluating your file. First impressions count.
